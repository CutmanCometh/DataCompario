DataCompareio

A simple tool for comparing two similar datasets to find discrepancies

A few constraints on your data (we're working on fixes and UX improvements):
  • You must have a unique identifier, and it must be in the first column.
  • Both datasets must have the same number of columns in each and every row.
  • The columns have to be in the same order, or else the comparison won't work.
  • Rows must be separated by a newline (unix or windows, doesn't matter), and values within a row should be separated by tabs.
